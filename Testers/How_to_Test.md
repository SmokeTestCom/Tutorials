# How to Test
1. Goto [smoke-test.com/GoTest](https://www.smoke-test.com/GoTest)
2. Read the test discription.
3. Try to do the steps described.
4. Provide instant feedback.
    * Fail, let your test experts know when a test has failed to produce the desigered outcome.
    * Flag, let your test experts know when the wording of a test is confusing, or the button moved to help keep the tests accurate and relevant.
    * Pass, the usecase worked like a charm.
5. If you simply want to try a different test, just "Skip Test".  Sometimes, you just need a change of pace.
